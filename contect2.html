Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. International shipping times vary by location but typically take 7-14 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn, undamaged items with original tags attached. Return shipping is free for US customers. International returns are subject to shipping fees.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. All customs fees and import duties are the responsibility of the customer.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number on our website or the carrier's website to monitor your package's progress.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer premium gift wrapping services for $5.95 per item. During checkout, select the gift wrapping option and include a personalized message. The packing slip will not include pricing information.
