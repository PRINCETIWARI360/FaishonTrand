Size Guide

Find your perfect fit with our comprehensive size guide. Learn how to measure yourself and choose the right size for every item.

How to Measure Yourself

For accurate measurements, wear form-fitting clothing and use a soft measuring tape. Follow these steps:

Chest/Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

Waist

Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist, usually above the belly button.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, approximately 8 inches below your waist.

Inseam

Measure from the top of your inner thigh down to the bottom of your ankle.

Size Selection Tips

Know Your Body Type

Different brands may fit differently based on your body shape. Know whether you're pear, apple, or hourglass shaped.

Check Fabric Stretch

Stretchy fabrics can accommodate up to one size smaller. Non-stretch fabrics need precise measurements.

Consider Garment Style

Fitted styles require exact measurements while loose styles can be sized up or down.

When in Doubt, Size Up

It's easier to take in a garment than to let it out. When between sizes, choose the larger size.
